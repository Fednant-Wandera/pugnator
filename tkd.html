  • The tenets of Taekwondo are often given in a list of five: courtesy, integrity, perseverance, self-control and indomitable spirit. Following recitation of the tenets, students then recite the student oath, as follows:
      Observe the tenets of Taekwondo.
      Respect all seniors and instructors.
      Never misuse Taekwondo.
      Be a champion of freedom and justice.
      Help to build a more peaceful world.
